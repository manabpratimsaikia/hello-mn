Very Short Questions (VSQs)

  • Define electric current.
    Answer: It is the rate of flow of electric charge through a conductor.
  • State Ohm‚Äôs law.
    Answer: V = IR, where V is voltage, I is current and R is resistance.
  • What is drift velocity?
    Answer: Average velocity with which free electrons drift under an electric field.
  • What is meant by EMF?
    Answer: Electromotive force; it is the energy supplied by a cell per coulomb of charge.
  • Define resistivity.
    Answer: It is the resistance offered by a conductor of unit length and unit cross-sectional area.
