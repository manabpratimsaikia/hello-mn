Important Questions - Chapter 9
Ray Optics and Optical Instruments

Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

  • Light travels fastest in:
    Answer: Vacuum
  • The mirror used in solar furnaces is:
    Answer: Concave mirror
  • The focal length of a plane mirror is:
    Answer: Infinity
  • Which lens is used to correct myopia?
    Answer: Concave lens
  • Power of a lens is measured in:
    Answer: Dioptre (D)

Very Short Questions (VSQs)

  • Define refractive index.
    Answer: Ratio of speed of light in vacuum to speed in medium
  • What is total internal reflection?
    Answer: Complete reflection of light inside a denser medium
  • Name one optical instrument.
    Answer: Microscope, Telescope, Camera etc.
  • What is the nature of image formed by convex mirror?
    Answer: Virtual, erect, and diminished
  • What is the magnification of a plane mirror?
    Answer: +1
