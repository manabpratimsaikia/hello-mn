Important Questions - Chapter 13
Nuclei

Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

  • Which particle has no charge and is found in the nucleus?
    Answer: Neutron
  • Mass defect is related to:
    Answer: Binding energy
  • Which reaction is responsible for energy production in the sun?
    Answer: Nuclear fusion
  • The binding energy per nucleon is maximum for:
    Answer: Iron (Fe)
  • 1 atomic mass unit (amu) equals:
    Answer: 931 MeV

Very Short Questions (VSQs)

  • Define mass defect.
    Answer: Difference between the sum of masses of nucleons and the actual mass of nucleus.
  • What is binding energy?
