Important Questions - Chapter 7
Alternating Current

Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

  • The frequency of AC in India is:
    Answer: 50 Hz
  • In pure resistive AC circuit, current and voltage are:
    Answer: In phase
  • Power factor of pure inductive or capacitive circuit is:
    Answer: Zero
  • What is the average value of AC over one full cycle?
    Answer: Zero
  • What is the relation between peak and RMS value of AC?
    Answer: Irms = I0/√2

Very Short Questions (VSQs)

  • Define RMS value of alternating current.
    Answer: It is the value of DC current that produces same heat as AC in same time
  • What is power factor?
    Answer: Cosine of phase angle between voltage and current
  • What is a choke coil?
    Answer: A coil with high inductance and low resistance used to control AC current
  • Write the expression of current in an LCR circuit.
    Answer: I = V / Z, where Z is impedance
  • What is the condition for resonance in LCR circuit?
    Answer: XL = XC or ωL = 1/ωC

Long Answer Questions

  • Derive the expression for current in an AC circuit containing only resistor.
    Answer: I = V0 sin(ωt)/R = I0 sin(ωt)
  • Derive the expression for average and RMS value of AC.
    Answer: Iavg = (2/π)I0, Irms = I0/√2
  • Explain the phasor diagram of RLC series circuit.
    Answer: Shows vector addition of voltage across R, L, and C; phase angle determines power factor
  • Explain resonance in series LCR circuit and its significance.
    Answer: Resonance occurs when XL = XC, current is maximum and impedance is minimum
  • Define power factor and derive expression for power in AC circuit.
    Answer: Power = VI cos(φ); cos(φ) is power factor
