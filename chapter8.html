Very Short Questions (VSQs)

  • Name the two components of electromagnetic wave.
    Answer: Electric field and Magnetic field
  • What is the source of electromagnetic waves?
    Answer: Accelerated charges
  • What is displacement current?
    Answer: Current due to time varying electric field
  • State one property of electromagnetic waves.
    Answer: They do not require any medium for propagation
  • Give an example of electromagnetic wave.
    Answer: Light, X-rays, radio waves etc.
