Important Questions - Chapter 6
Electromagnetic Induction

Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

  • Who discovered electromagnetic induction?
    Answer: Michael Faraday
  • Which law governs electromagnetic induction?
    Answer: Faraday’s Law
  • Lenz's law is in accordance with which principle?
    Answer: Law of conservation of energy
  • Induced emf is directly proportional to:
    Answer: Rate of change of magnetic flux
  • The SI unit of magnetic flux is:
    Answer: Weber (Wb)

Very Short Questions (VSQs)

  • What is electromagnetic induction?
    Answer: Generation of emf due to change in magnetic flux
  • State Lenz’s law.
    Answer: The direction of induced emf is such that it opposes the cause producing it
  • Define magnetic flux.
    Answer: Total number of magnetic field lines passing through an area
  • What is the unit of magnetic flux?
    Answer: Weber (Wb)
  • Write Faraday’s second law.
    Answer: Induced emf is equal to the negative rate of change of magnetic flux

Long Answer Questions

  • State and explain Faraday’s laws of electromagnetic induction.
    Answer: Faraday’s first law states that emf is induced when magnetic flux changes. Second law gives emf = -dΦ/dt
  • Derive an expression for emf induced in a rod moving in a magnetic field.
    Answer: emf = B·l·v, where l is length, v is velocity, B is magnetic field
  • Explain Lenz’s law with an example.
    Answer: When a magnet is pushed into a coil, current opposes the motion. This demonstrates conservation of energy.
  • Derive expression for energy stored in an inductor.
    Answer: U = (1/2)·L·I², where L is inductance and I is current
  • Explain the concept of self and mutual induction.
    Answer: Self-induction is emf in same coil; mutual induction is emf induced in a different coil
