Very Short Questions (VSQs)

  • Define electric potential.
    Answer: Work done per unit charge in bringing a test charge from infinity to that point.
  • What is an equipotential surface?
    Answer: A surface where all points have the same electric potential.
  • Define 1 volt.
    Answer: Potential difference when 1 joule of work is done in moving 1 coulomb of charge.
  • What is the unit of capacitance?
    Answer: Farad (F)
